## Value Proposition Section

### Section Title: Why Houston Chooses Hail Lions PDR

**Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is the modern, cost-effective alternative to traditional body shop repairs.** Our certified technicians use specialized tools to massage dents from behind the panel, preserving your factory paint and maintaining your vehicle's value.

### Benefits Grid

#### Save Your Factory Paint
Unlike conventional body shops that sand, fill, and repaint, PDR maintains your original factory finish. No color matching issues, no overspray, and no risk of paint failure down the road.

#### 60% Faster Than Body Shops
Most repairs completed in 1-3 hours versus days at a traditional body shop. Get back on the road quickly with our efficient mobile and in-shop services.

#### Insurance Preferred Method
All major insurance companies prefer PDR for hail and minor dent repairs. We work directly with your insurer, handling the paperwork so you don't have to worry.

#### Lifetime Warranty
Every repair backed by our comprehensive lifetime warranty. If the dent ever reappears, we'll fix it free—guaranteed.
