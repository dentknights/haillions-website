## FAQ Section

### H2: Hail Damage Repair FAQ

#### How much does hail damage repair cost?
Hail damage repair costs vary based on the number and severity of dents. Minor hail damage might cost $500-$1,500, while severe storms causing hundreds of dents can range $2,000-$8,000+. Most comprehensive insurance policies cover hail damage after your deductible. We provide free assessments and work with all insurance companies.

#### How long does hail damage repair take?
Most hail damage repairs are completed in 1-3 business days. The timeframe depends on the extent of damage—a vehicle with 20-30 dents might take 1 day, while a car with 100+ dents could take 2-3 days. We provide a specific timeline during your free assessment.

#### Will my insurance rates go up if I file a hail claim?
Hail damage is typically considered a "comprehensive" claim, which is different from an "at-fault" collision claim. Comprehensive claims for weather damage usually don't impact your rates. However, we recommend checking with your specific insurance provider for confirmation.

#### Can all hail damage be repaired with PDR?
Most hail damage can be repaired using paintless dent repair. However, if the paint is cracked or the metal is severely stretched, conventional repair may be necessary. During your free assessment, we'll evaluate every dent and provide honest recommendations.

#### Do you offer rental car assistance?
Many insurance policies include rental car coverage for hail damage repairs. We can help coordinate with your insurance company and have partnerships with local rental agencies if needed.

#### What if my car was totaled by hail damage?
If your insurance company declares your vehicle a total loss, you have options. Sometimes the "salvage" value makes keeping and repairing the vehicle economical. We can assess whether PDR makes financial sense for your situation.

#### Can you match paint if PDR isn't possible?
While PDR preserves your factory paint, some severe hail damage may require conventional repair. In these cases, we partner with premium body shops that specialize in computer-matched paint systems for perfect color matching.

#### Is there a warranty on hail damage repairs?
Yes! All our hail damage repairs come with a lifetime warranty. If any repaired dent reappears, we'll fix it at no charge.
