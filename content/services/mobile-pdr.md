## Mobile Service Requirements

### H2: What You Need for Mobile Service

#### Space Requirements
- **Covered area preferred** - Garage, carport, or covered parking
- **Minimum space** - Enough room to open doors fully
- **Flat surface** - Level ground for safe equipment setup
- **Power access** - Standard outlet within 50 feet (for LED lighting)

#### Weather Considerations
- **Rain** - We can work in light rain with covered areas; heavy rain may require rescheduling
- **Extreme heat** - Houston summers are challenging; covered areas essential
- **Wind** - High winds can affect our lighting systems

#### Vehicle Preparation
- Please have your vehicle clean enough to assess damage
- Ensure we can access all damaged areas
- Have keys available for interior access if needed

## Mobile vs. Shop Service

### H2: Which Should You Choose?

#### Choose Mobile Service When:
- You have 1-5 dents or dings
- Damage is accessible (doors, fenders, hood, trunk)
- You have a covered area available
- Convenience is a priority
- You have a busy schedule

#### Choose Shop Service When:
- You have extensive hail damage (50+ dents)
- Damage requires bumper or panel removal
- You need additional services (detailing, paint touch-up)
- Weather conditions aren't suitable for mobile
- You prefer our controlled shop environment

## FAQ Section

### H2: Mobile PDR FAQ

#### How much does mobile dent repair cost?
Mobile service pricing is comparable to our shop rates, with a modest trip fee ($25-$50 depending on distance). Most door dings cost $75-$150. We provide exact quotes before beginning work.

#### How do I schedule mobile service?
Call (713) 555-HAIL or book online. We'll confirm your location, assess your damage via photos if possible, and schedule a convenient appointment time.

#### What areas do you serve?
We provide mobile service throughout Greater Houston within approximately 50 miles of downtown. This includes all major suburbs: Sugar Land, Katy, The Woodlands, Pearland, Cypress, Spring, Humble, Kingwood, Clear Lake, and Missouri City.

#### Do I need to be present during the repair?
You don't need to watch the entire process, but we do need someone available to provide vehicle access and approve the completed work. Many customers have us repair their vehicle while they work from home or the office.

#### What if it rains during my mobile appointment?
We need a covered area (garage, carport, covered parking) to work in wet weather. If no covered area is available and rain is forecast, we'll contact you to reschedule.

#### Is mobile service as good as shop service?
Absolutely! Our mobile technicians use the same tools, techniques, and quality standards as our shop. Many repairs are actually better suited to mobile service.

#### Can you do hail damage repair mobile?
For minor hail damage (under 20 dents), mobile service often works well. Extensive hail damage with hundreds of dents is typically better handled at our shop for efficiency.

#### Do you offer mobile service for businesses?
Yes! We provide fleet services for businesses throughout Houston. Keep your company vehicles looking professional without taking them off the road. Contact us for fleet pricing.

#### What forms of payment do you accept for mobile service?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and checks. Payment is processed on-site after you approve the completed repair.

#### Is there a warranty on mobile repairs?
Yes, all mobile repairs carry our lifetime warranty, just like shop repairs. If any issue arises, we'll make it right.
